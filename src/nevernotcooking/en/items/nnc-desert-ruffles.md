  Black-and-white fashion editorial photographed across a roadside landscape in California. The series contrasts structured garments with transient environments—motorcycles, signage, asphalt, neon.

  The work operates as a study in visual storytelling: fabric, posture, and light create a controlled narrative inside uncontrolled space. Each frame isolates the subject while keeping the surrounding context legible—editorial design translated into image composition.

  Positioned between content creation and digital content production, the series explores how fashion imagery can function as a narrative system rather than a single image outcome.
